Aahil's cake recipe

Ingredients:-

  • ½ tin Nestlé MILKMAID
  • ½ cup NESTLÉ a+ Toned Milk
  • Nestlé a+ Dahi 100g
  • Maida 50 g
  • Cocoa Powder 1 tsp
  • Baking soda

HOW TO MAKE CHOCOLATE FUDGE CAKE

  1. Step 1 Grease and dust an 8” baking tin and preheat oven to 180° C. Sieve together maida, cocoa & baking soda.
  2. Step 2 Beat butter and dahi until light and creamy. Add Nestlé MILKMAID and blend. Fold in the sieved ingredients, alternating with milk and pour into greased tin.
  3. Step 3 Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until tooth pick inserted in the centre comes out clean. Allow to cool in tin for 5 minutes and remove onto a wire rack.
  4. Step 4 For chocolate icing, heat all the all icing ingredients together till it leaves the sides of the pan. Glaze cake and serve when cool!
